Elpriset is a Swedish electricity price app that displays real-time spot prices with total cost calculations (grid fees, energy tax, VAT).

No login or account is required. The app fetches publicly available electricity price data from elprisetjustnu.se and Energi Data Service APIs.

To test the app:
1. Launch the app — it will show the onboarding flow
2. Select any electricity zone (SE1-SE4) or allow location access for automatic detection
3. The dashboard shows current price, today's price chart (96 quarter-hour bars), and smart tips
4. Pull down to refresh price data
5. Widgets can be added to the home screen or lock screen

The app works with real price data — no test account or special configuration needed.
